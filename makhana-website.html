OUR STORY

From the Sacred Ponds
of Mithilanchal

Bihar's Mithila region has cultivated makhana (Euryale ferox) for over 500 years in the wetlands of Darbhanga, Madhubani and Muzaffarpur. Each lotus seed is hand-harvested by skilled farmers, sun-dried on flat riverbanks, then popped over open fire — a craft passed down through generations.

We work directly with 200+ farmer families, paying fair prices and bringing you the freshest makhana within 2 weeks of harvest — no cold storage, no middlemen.
